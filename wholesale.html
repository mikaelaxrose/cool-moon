WHOLESALE

Cool Moon’s ice creams are hand-made right in our store kitchen. Our ice creams are cane sugar sweetened and made with 100% natural ingredients. Ice creams all contain eggs. Our ice cream “Life List” includes close to 200 flavors. We offer our super-premium ice creams and sorbets to restaurants, caterers, and our customers generally, in larger quantities on a wholesale basis. Choose from among the flavors we have in regular production, or we can work with you to customize a special flavor. All custom and large orders require advance notice of at least one week. Please call or email us to discuss specifics regarding flavors, amounts, production schedule and pricing. Wholesale pricing varies by flavor, so we can better help you if you give us flavor information when you contact us. We are always happy to discuss our flavors and offer our suggestions!
